What is Open Ports?

Open Ports is a Python-based TCP port scanner designed to identify active services running on a target system. It provides quick insights into the exposed network surface of hosts through efficient multithreaded scanning.

Why Use It?

Whether you're learning cybersecurity, troubleshooting network issues, or performing authorized security assessments, Open Ports helps you discover accessible services and understand potential attack vectors.

Built For

Students, cybersecurity enthusiasts, ethical hackers, and system administrators who need a lightweight and easy-to-use scanning utility without external dependencies.

Features

Multithreaded

Scans hundreds of ports simultaneously for faster results.

Service Detection

Automatically identifies common services running on open ports.

Statistics

Displays scan duration, open ports found and complete summaries.

Usage 

python3 port_scanner.py TARGET START_PORT END_PORT

Example:

python3 port_scanner.py google.com 1 1000
